GlassSleuth Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026

GlassSleuth identifies vintage glass entirely on your device. This policy explains what the app does — and, mostly, what it doesn't do.

Data we collect

None. GlassSleuth has no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no servers of ours that receive your data. Identification runs on your iPhone using an on-device model — the photos you scan are never uploaded anywhere.

Where your data lives

  • On your device. Your scans, saved finds, collection, and any prices you record are stored locally.
  • Nowhere else. We run no backend. If you delete the app, that data is gone with it (standard iOS device backups aside).

Photos and camera

Camera and photo-library access is used only to identify glass and attach the picture to a saved find. Photos never leave your device.

Purchases

Subscriptions and the lifetime unlock are processed by Apple through the App Store. We receive no payment details. Apple's privacy policy governs that transaction.

Value estimates

Value ranges shown in the app are editorial estimates bundled with the app. Requesting or displaying them involves no transmission of your data.

Children

GlassSleuth is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version will be posted at this address with an updated effective date.
