Frequently asked

How accurate are identifications?

GlassSleuth is honest about confidence. When the model is sure, you get a best match; when it isn't, you get "similar matches" to pick from — never a confident wrong answer. Identification quality keeps improving with app updates as the catalog grows.

Are the value ranges appraisals?

No. They're editorial estimates of realistic resale ranges for pieces in good condition (no chips, cracks, or dishwasher fade), based on real sold prices. Rare pieces deserve professional authentication — the app says so when it matters.

Does it work offline?

Yes — identification runs entirely on your iPhone. Basement estate sales are exactly the point.

Why does the app say a piece is "likely modern"?

Because that's the honest answer. Reproductions (like modern jadeite) and new giftware are common at thrift stores, and telling them apart from vintage is the app's job.

How do I restore my Pro purchase on a new phone?

Settings → Restore purchases, signed into the same Apple Account.

How do I cancel?

App Store app → your profile → Subscriptions → GlassSleuth Pro → Cancel. You keep Pro until the period ends. The lifetime unlock is a one-time purchase — nothing to cancel.
